# AlphaScroll 🚀
The ultimate hands-free automatic scroller for YouTube Shorts. You could now relax both your mind and your hands while scrolling. 
## How to Install for Free 🛠️
1. Click the green **Code** button at the top right of this repository page and select **Download ZIP**.
2. Unzip the downloaded folder onto your Mac Desktop.
3. Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
4. In the top-right corner, flip the switch to turn **Developer mode** ON.
5. In the top-left corner, click the **Load unpacked** button.
6. Select your extracted project folder (`Alphascroll`) from your Desktop and click Open.
7. Open YouTube Shorts, click your new pinned Rocket icon, hit **Activate**, and enjoy hands-free viewing!
